Several online tools for single-cell datasets in human and mouse skeletal muscle have been previously published. Check them out below.

Human Skeletal Muscle Aging Atlas

Kedlian et al. Nature Aging (2024)
Tool: muscleageingcellatlas.org

HLMA: Human Muscle Ageing Cell Atlas

Lai et al. Nature (2024)
Tool: db.cngb.org/cdcp/hlma/

The Single-Cell Muscle Project (scMuscle)

McKellar et al. Commun Biol. (2021)
Tool: scmuscle.bme.cornell.edu

We are working on providing an integrated view of those datasets. Stay tuned!
