Work in Progress

This section includes experimental apps. Expect bugs and incomplete features!
We appreciate your feedback — please contact us with issues or suggestions.

Template App

A template providing minimal R and HTML structure for integrating new apps into the repository.

Mouse High-fat Diet

Transcriptomics analysis of skeletal muscle, adipose tissue and liver in mice exposed to high-fat diet.

Diabetic Myocytes

Transcriptome of myoblasts and myotubes grown in vitro from individuals with or without type 2 diabetes.

Myotube Heterogeneity

Transcriptome of primary skeletal myotubes processed at different times with various methods.

Mouse Exercise

Transcriptomics analysis of skeletal muscle, adipose tissue and liver in mice after acute exercise.

Single-cell RNAseq

Exercise response in skeletal muscle analyzed by single-cell RNAseq from Lovrić et al, 2022.

Human Clamp

Transcriptomic response of skeletal muscle response to an hyperinsulinemic euglycemic clamp.

In vitro EPS

Transcriptomic response to electrical pulse stimulation in human primary and mouse C2C12 myotubes.
