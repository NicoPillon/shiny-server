Skeletal Muscle Omics (Beta)

Dive into dynamic, interactive tools to explore how skeletal muscle adapts to exercise, aging, metabolic disease, and more.

Exercise

Transcriptomic profiling of skeletal muscle adaptations to exercise and inactivity (MetaMEx).

Aging

Human skeletal muscle transcriptome throughout the lifespan and its alterations in sarcopenia.

Obesity

Transcriptomics analysis of skeletal muscle in men and women with overweight, obesity or type 2 diabetes.

Fiber Type

Profiling of the proteome and transcriptome of human skeletal muscle type I and type II fibers.

Muscle Models

Transcriptome of C2C12, L6 and human primary myotubes compared to skeletal muscle tissue.

Global VO2max

Worldwide reference values for maximal oxygen uptake in 20 to 80 years old men and women.

Work in Progress

This section includes experimental apps. Expect bugs and incomplete features!

Mouse High-fat Diet

Transcriptomics analysis of skeletal muscle, adipose tissue and liver in mice exposed to high-fat diet.

Diabetic Myocytes

Transcriptome of myoblasts and myotubes grown in vitro from individuals with or without type 2 diabetes.

Palmitate in Myotubes

Transcriptomic response of skeletal myotubes exposed to the saturated fatty acid palmitate in vitro.

Myotube Heterogeneity

Transcriptome of primary skeletal myotubes processed at different times with various methods.

Mouse Exercise

Transcriptomics analysis of skeletal muscle, adipose tissue and liver in mice after acute exercise.

Single-cell RNAseq

Exercise response in skeletal muscle analyzed by single-cell RNAseq from Lovrić et al, 2022.

Human Clamp

Transcriptomic response of skeletal muscle response to an hyperinsulinemic euglycemic clamp.

In vitro EPS

Transcriptomic response to electrical pulse stimulation in human primary and mouse C2C12 myotubes.
