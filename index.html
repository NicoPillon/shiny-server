Dive into interactive tools to explore how skeletal muscle adapts to exercise, aging, metabolic disease, and more.

Start by exploring the overview, or pick a specific dataset below.

Exploring muscle omics often involves leveraging external resources. We've assembled a collection of helpful databases and utilities to streamline your experience. You're just a click away from valuable add-ons.

The Muscle Community

This project is rooted in the collaborative spirit of the scientific community. We recognize the tremendous contributions of researchers worldwide who have laid the foundation for understanding muscle biology. Our goal is to support ongoing discovery by keeping this platform open, evolving, and useful.


We are always open to ideas, partnerships, and improvements. If you have feedback or suggestions, don't hesitate to reach out!
